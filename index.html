Sharks Aren't That Bad

Sharks are big fish. They're ectothermic, live in water, have fins, and breathe with gills, like most fish. One of the main differences is that a shark's skeleton is made of cartilage and not bone.

There are 500 different species of sharks. 143 of those shark species range from being vulnerable to critically endangered.

Sharks have been around for a really long time.
